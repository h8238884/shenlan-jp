Kamakura & Enoshima (Custom)

Temples, Enoden, and sea breeze. A slower-paced photoshoot with coastal mood.

Sample itinerary

Classic Kamakura

Kamakura Station → Komachi-dori → Tsurugaoka Hachimangu

  • Komachi-dori street (snacks / casual portraits)
  • Tsurugaoka Hachimangu (grand stairs + shrine vibe)
  • Quiet backstreets for cleaner frames
Hase area

Great Buddha (Kotoku-in) → Hasedera

  • Iconic Great Buddha shot (timing matters)
  • Hasedera temple views + seasonal flowers
  • Short walks with soft light (great for couples)
Coast & Enoden

Enoden + Shichirigahama / Inamuragasaki (sunset)

  • Enoden atmosphere (retro local train)
  • Coastline portraits + wind + silhouettes
  • Optional: Enoshima island for golden hour
Best for
  • Couples / proposals
  • Family day trip from Tokyo
  • Film-like seaside portraits
Notes
  • Weekends get crowded: we adjust order and timing to avoid the worst rush.
  • If visibility is good, we can frame Mt. Fuji from the coast at sunset.
  • Route can be split: “temples-focused” or “sea-focused”.
