Daikoku PA JDM Car Meet (Custom)

One of the most iconic car meet spots in the Tokyo/Yokohama area. Perfect for travelers who want JDM culture + night vibes.

Highlights

JDM cars · Night views · Shuto Expressway vibes · Car meet atmosphere · Photo-friendly

Duration: ~3 hoursBest on weekendsAccess can vary
What’s included
  • 3-hour itinerary (adjusted by season/traffic/on-site conditions)
  • Route planning + on-site guidance for car meet viewing
  • Optional short photo stops when safe and permitted
Good for
  • JDM / car culture fans
  • Travelers who want Tokyo night vibes
  • Anyone looking for a light, photo-friendly car meet experience
Important
  • Daikoku PA is inside the expressway system; access and crowds are not guaranteed.
  • If restricted/closed, we’ll switch to alternative spots (e.g., Umihotaru PA) + night route.
  • Please follow local rules and safety guidance at all times.
