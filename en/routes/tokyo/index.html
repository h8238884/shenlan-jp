Tokyo (Custom Photoshoot)

From shrine calm to neon night. We design the route by season, crowds, and your style (clean / cinematic / street).

Sample itinerary

West Tokyo · Classic

Meiji Jingu → Harajuku → Cat Street → Shibuya

  • Morning calm at Meiji Shrine & forest paths
  • Harajuku Takeshita Street (pop / kawaii)
  • Cat Street walk (boutiques + cafes)
  • Hachiko + Shibuya Scramble Crossing (iconic)
  • Optional: Shibuya Sky sunset (if you book tickets)
Old Tokyo · Traditional

Asakusa (Kimono-friendly) + Riverside views

  • Kaminarimon gate & Senso-ji area
  • Nakamise street (snacks / souvenirs)
  • Backstreets for cleaner portraits
  • Sumida River / Skytree in background
Night · Cinematic

Shinjuku / Shibuya neon (rainy nights are great)

  • Kabukicho neon + cinematic frames
  • Omoide Yokocho / small alleys for mood
  • Street-light portraits (film look)
  • Alternative: Ginza / Tokyo Station night vibe
Best for
  • Couples, solo, friends, family
  • Kimono / casual street / proposal
  • First-time Tokyo visitors
Notes
  • Timing matters: we avoid peak crowds and chase the best light.
  • We can start from your hotel (central Tokyo) or meet at the first location.
  • If it rains: we use covered arcades / reflections for a stronger cinematic look.
